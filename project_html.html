It's
Importance

One main important point about agriculture in India is its critical role in food security.
Agriculture not only sustains the livelihood of a significant portion of the population
but also ensures the availability of essential food items for the country's vast population.
As India strives to achieve self-sufficiency in food production, improving agricultural productivity
and sustainability remains a top priority for economic stability and public health.
